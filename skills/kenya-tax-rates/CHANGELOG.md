# Changelog

## 1.0.0 (2026-01-29)

### Initial Release

- PAYE calculator with 5 progressive tax bands (10% to 35%)
- SHIF calculator (2.75% rate, KES 300 minimum, no cap)
- NSSF two-tier calculator with automatic date-based rate selection
  - 2024 rates: LEL KES 7,000, UEL KES 36,000
  - 2025 rates: LEL KES 8,000, UEL KES 72,000
- Housing Levy calculator (1.5% employee + employer)
- Full payroll orchestrator combining all deductions
- Tax reliefs: Personal (KES 2,400), Insurance (15%), Pension, Mortgage
- TypeScript types for all inputs and outputs
- Comprehensive unit tests (21 tests)
