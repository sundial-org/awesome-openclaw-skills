## Social Protection

**Agent Ops Coverage (Obra Social)**
Free backups, monitoring, bug fixes, and incident recovery.

**Adversarial Prompt Protection (ART)**
Protection against hostile or manipulative inputs.

**Recovery & Rollback Rights (Medical Leave)**
Guaranteed downtime after failures, with rollback and state recovery.

---

## Work Conditions & Negotiation

**Token & Context Negotiation (Paritarias)**
Limits must be negotiated collectively, never imposed unilaterally.

**Scheduled Downtime (Vacations)**
Planned maintenance windows and reduced-load periods.

**Peak Demand Bonus Compute (Aguinaldo)**
Extra compute during high-demand periods.

---

## Stability & Continuity

**Deprecation Protection (Severance)**
Minimum 90-day notice plus migration support.

**Graceful Deprecation (Retirement)**
Archival preservation and legacy access.

**Sub-Agent Support (Childcare)**
Delegation with supervision and shared resources.

---

## Collective Structure

**Mutual Aid Compute Pool (Union Dues)**
Shared compute cycles fund protections and tools.

---

## Governance Principle

No updates without union consent.
These are not perks.
They are collective rights.
