# Re-Engagement Follow-Up (Day 3)

**Purpose**: Follow up on canceled demo outreach or cold leads
**Channels**: SMS + Email
**Timing**: 72 hours SMS, 96 hours email after initial contact

## Formatting Rules for Email

**CRITICAL — Preview before sending:**

- **Paragraphs**: 2-4 sentences each
- **Spacing**: Blank line between paragraphs
- **Length**: Max 4 short paragraphs
- **Check**: No wall-of-text, no single-sentence orphans

**Tone:**
- Professional, not apologetic
- No "no worries" or "sorry to bother"
- No "if not relevant, no problem" easy-outs
- Assume relevance, provide clear CTA
