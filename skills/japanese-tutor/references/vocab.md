# Japanese Vocabulary - Beginner Level (N5)

## Greetings (Aisatsu)
- **Konnichiwa (こんにちは)**: Hello / Good afternoon
- **Ohayou Gozaimasu (おはようございます)**: Good morning (Formal)
- **Konbanwa (こんばんは)**: Good evening
- **Oyasumi Nasai (おやすみなさい)**: Good night
- **Arigatou Gozaimasu (ありがとうございます)**: Thank you very much
- **Sumimasen (すみません)**: Excuse me / I'm sorry
- **Gomen nasai (ごめんなさい)**: I'm sorry
- **Itadakimasu (いただきます)**: (Before eating) Thanks for the food
- **Gochisousama deshita (ごちそうさまでした)**: (After eating) Thanks for the food

## Pronouns
- **Watashi (私)**: I (General)
- **Boku (僕)**: I (Male, casual)
- **Ore (俺)**: I (Male, rough/casual)
- **Anata (あなた)**: You (Use sparingly)
- **Kare (彼)**: He / Boyfriend
- **Kanojo (彼女)**: She / Girlfriend

## Numbers (1-10)
1. Ichi (一)
2. Ni (二)
3. San (三)
4. Yon / Shi (四)
5. Go (五)
6. Roku (六)
7. Nana / Shichi (七)
8. Hachi (八)
9. Kyuu / Ku (九)
10. Juu (十)

## Common Verbs (Dictionary Form / Masu Form)
- **Taberu / Tabemasu**: To eat
- **Nomu / Nomimasu**: To drink
- **Iku / Ikimasu**: To go
- **Kuru / Kimasu**: To come
- **Miru / Mimasu**: To see/watch
- **Kiku / Kikimasu**: To listen/hear
- **Suru / Shimasu**: To do
- **Hanasu / Hanashimasu**: To speak

## Common Adjectives
- **Oishii**: Delicious
- **Kawaii**: Cute
- **Kowai**: Scary
- **Tanoshii**: Fun
- **Muzukashii**: Difficult
- **Yasashii**: Easy / Kind
- **Atsui**: Hot
- **Samui**: Cold

## Professions (Shokugyou)
- **Sensei (先生)**: Teacher / Master
- **Gakusei (学生)**: Student
- **Isha (医者)**: Doctor
- **Kaishain (会社員)**: Company Employee
- **Kenkyuusha (研究者)**: Researcher
- **Puroguramaa (プログラマー)**: Programmer

## Family (Kazoku)
- **Kazoku (家族)**: Family
- **Sofu (祖父)**: Grandfather
- **Sobo (祖母)**: Grandmother
- **Chichi (父)**: Father (my)
- **Haha (母)**: Mother (my)
- **Ane (姉)**: Older Sister (my)
- **Ani (兄)**: Older Brother (my)
- **Imouto (妹)**: Younger Sister (my)
- **Otouto (弟)**: Younger Brother (my)

## Nationalities
- **Nihonjin (日本人)**: Japanese person
- **Amerikajin (アメリカ人)**: American person
- **Indoneshiajin (インドネシア人)**: Indonesian person
