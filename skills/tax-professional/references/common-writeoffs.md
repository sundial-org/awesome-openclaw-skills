# Common Write-Offs People Miss

## Frequently Overlooked Deductions

### For W-2 Employees
- **HSA contributions** — Triple tax advantage (deductible, grows tax-free, withdraws tax-free for medical)
- **Student loan interest** — Even if someone else pays it (if you're the borrower)
- **Job search expenses** — In same field (but note: suspended 2018-2025 for W-2)
- **State sales tax** — Can elect to deduct sales tax instead of state income tax (Schedule A)

### For Self-Employed / Side Hustlers
- **Self-employment tax deduction** — 50% of SE tax is above-the-line
- **Health insurance premiums** — 100% deductible above-the-line if SE
- **Retirement contributions** — SEP-IRA or Solo 401(k) are massive deductions
- **Cell phone** — Business use percentage
- **Internet** — Business use percentage
- **Home office** — Even a corner of a room counts if exclusively used
- **Bank fees & merchant fees** — PayPal, Stripe, bank account fees
- **Continuing education** — Must be in current field
- **Business miles** — Track EVERY trip. Apps: MileIQ, Everlance, manual log
- **Startup costs** — Up to $5,000 deductible in first year, amortize the rest

### For Everyone
- **Charitable mileage** — 14¢/mile, most people forget
- **Glasses and contacts** — Medical expense (if itemizing and exceeding 7.5% AGI)
- **Tax preparation fees** — For Schedule C filers, business portion is deductible
- **Moving expenses** — Only for active-duty military (suspended for others 2018-2025)
- **Gambling losses** — Deductible up to amount of gambling winnings (must itemize)
- **Casualty & theft losses** — Only in federally declared disaster areas (2018-2025)

### Investment-Related
- **Investment advisory fees** — Suspended for 2018-2025 (may return in 2026!)
- **Margin interest** — Deductible against investment income
- **Capital loss harvesting** — $3,000/year against ordinary income
- **Worthless securities** — Deduct full loss in year stock becomes worthless

### AI & Tech Worker Specific
- **Cloud computing costs** — AWS, GCP, Azure for business/freelance
- **API costs** — OpenAI, Anthropic, etc. if used for business
- **Domain names & hosting** — Business websites
- **SaaS subscriptions** — Business tools (Notion, GitHub, Figma, etc.)
- **Hardware** — Laptops, monitors, keyboards, mice, webcams
- **Conference tickets** — Tech conferences, AI summits
- **Online courses** — Udemy, Coursera, etc. if work-related
- **Books & publications** — Technical books, O'Reilly subscription
