# Outline Templates

## Deep Analysis Article Template

```
ARTICLE TITLE: [Specific, question-based or claim-based]

OPENING STRATEGY: [Choose one]
- Shocking observation about [topic]
- Rhetorical question: [question that frames the piece]
- Direct assertion: "Well, [observation that challenges conventional wisdom]"

HOOK (1-2 sentences):
[Opening that captures reader attention and establishes stakes]

THESIS STATEMENT:
[Core argument in 1-2 sentences]

---

SECTION I: CONTEXT & BACKGROUND
- Why this matters now
- Current industry narrative
- What most people believe about this

SECTION II: THE EVIDENCE
- Key data point #1 with source
- Key data point #2 with source  
- Key data point #3 with source
- What the data actually shows vs. what's claimed

SECTION III: THE PROBLEMATIC ASSUMPTION
- What assumption underlies the narrative
- Why this assumption is shaky
- Specific examples of where it breaks down

SECTION IV: THE REAL IMPLICATIONS
- What happens if this continues
- Who benefits (and who doesn't)
- Connected trends that amplify this

SECTION V: COUNTERARGUMENTS & NUANCE
- Legitimate point from opposing view
- Where your skepticism might be premature
- Acknowledging what you don't fully understand

CLOSING:
- Sharp observation that ties to opening
- Implied question left with reader
- What this means going forward

---

TONE NOTES:
- Maintain skepticism but intellectual humility
- Use [X] specific examples
- Include [Y] pieces of supporting evidence
- Incorporate [Z] rhetorical questions
```
